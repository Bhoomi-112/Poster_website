Current Limitations of AI

Bias: AI can inherit and amplify biases present in training data.

Common Sense: AI often lacks true understanding and real-world reasoning.

Explainability: Complex models ("black boxes") can be hard to interpret.

Robustness: Susceptible to adversarial attacks (small, malicious input changes).
